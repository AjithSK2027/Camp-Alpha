Where Untamed Forest Harmony Meets Refined Luxury.

Cradled in the majestic hills of Chikkamagaluru within the historic Lourdes Estate, Camp Alpha is a sanctuary meticulously crafted for those seeking an sophisticated, off-grid refuge. Surrounded by centuries-old coffee plantations and intersected by a pristine, crystalline mountain river, our private homestay balances modern architectural design with raw, untouched forest landscapes.

Awaken to the crisp, complex aroma of ripening Arabica beans, pass afternoons trackside tracking local wildlife through emerald clearings, or simply disconnect at our natural river stream. This isn't just an itinerary; it's an elevated communion with nature.
