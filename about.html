Our Legacy

The Tale of Lourdes Estate

Rooted deeply within the premium shade-grown coffee belts of Aldur, Chikkamagaluru, Camp Alpha was envisioned as an architectural escape from urban intensity, ensuring complete immersion in the local landscape.

Our operational philosophies honor the delicate balance of nature. We employ fully integrated water restoration loops and native agricultural methodologies, preserving the surrounding biodiversity so that future generations can witness the pristine wonders of the Western Ghats.
